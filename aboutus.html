About Us

We Use High Quality Fresh Ingredients!

Rainie Liu and Xin Sont immigrated from Changchun, China to Iowa in 2010. In 2019, they started Dumpling House, because “they noticed a lack of dumplings made their way they know and love them: a couple tablespoons of minced meat mixed with vegetables cocooned in chewy flour skin and pan-fried golden and crispy on the bottom” (Wignall).

Our dumplings are all handmade, from the wrapper to the filling. We purchase high-quality ingredients; meat, flour and carefully prepared to perfection for you with traditional steps and traditional ingredients. Our dumplings are freshly made, and we try our best to keep the food fresh and delicious for you always.
